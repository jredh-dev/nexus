# nascent-nexus

**Personal AI Assistant System**

A modular personal AI assistant that acts as your digital mind - remembering, reminding, and coordinating across all your services.

## Overview

nascent-nexus is a flexible integration hub and personal assistant system designed to:

- Connect to and coordinate between multiple services
- Provide SMS-based access for maximum ubiquity
- Act as your personal secretary across platforms
- Enable voice chat and community interaction
- Support recording and memory preservation
