DEADMAN SWITCH SERVICE — PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: 2026-03-08

1. WHAT THIS SERVICE IS

The Deadman Switch is a personal SMS alert service. An owner registers a check-in
schedule. If the owner stops checking in, the service sends SMS notifications to
designated subscribers.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTED

We collect only what is required to operate the service:

  - Phone numbers of owners, subscribers, and administrators
  - Optional display names provided at registration
  - Timestamps of check-ins and subscription events
  - Inbound SMS message content (the first character is used to route commands;
    full message content is logged for debugging)

We do not collect: location data, device identifiers, browsing history, or any
information beyond what you explicitly send via SMS.

3. HOW INFORMATION IS USED

Phone numbers and names are used exclusively to:
  - Send check-in reminders and trigger alerts
  - Route inbound SMS commands (R, W, H, U, Y, N, Q)
  - Notify administrators of subscriber polls (W and H commands)

We do not sell, share, or disclose your information to third parties except as
required to transmit SMS (Twilio, Inc., acting as our SMS carrier).

4. SMS CONSENT

Subscribers receive an initial consent SMS before being enrolled. Subscribing
requires an explicit Y reply. You may decline (N) or block all messages (Q) at
any time.

After enrolling, you may unsubscribe at any time by texting U to the service
number.

5. DATA RETENTION

Data is retained for as long as you are registered with the service. You may
request deletion of your phone number and associated records by contacting the
service administrator.

6. THIRD PARTIES

SMS delivery is handled by Twilio, Inc. (twilio.com). Twilio's privacy policy
applies to message transmission. No other third parties receive your data.

7. SECURITY

Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database on privately hosted infrastructure.
Twilio credentials are stored in HashiCorp Vault and are never written to disk
in plaintext. Access is restricted to the service administrator.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
  - Know what data we hold about you
  - Request correction of inaccurate data
  - Request deletion of your data
  - Opt out of SMS communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact the service administrator directly.

9. CONTACT

This service is operated by its registered owner. For questions or data
requests, contact the administrator via the service's registered contact
information.

10. CHANGES

This policy may be updated. The current version is always available at the
service's /privacy endpoint.
